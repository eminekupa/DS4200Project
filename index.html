Mediterranean Hotel Dynamics

Understanding Cancellation Behaviors Across France, Spain, and Italy

Introduction

Hotel booking cancellations represent a significant challenge for the hospitality industry, directly impacting revenue management and operational planning. Our project examines the patterns and factors influencing cancellation behaviors specifically across Mediterranean countries - Spain, France, and Italy - which are among Europe's most popular tourist destinations.

Using a comprehensive dataset of over 22,000 hotel bookings from both city and resort properties, we analyze how different guest demographics, booking channels, and loyalty factors affect cancellation likelihood. This regional focus allows us to identify meaningful patterns within similar yet distinct hospitality markets that share Mediterranean tourism characteristics.
