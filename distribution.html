Distribution Channel Impact

How Booking Methods Influence Hotel Cancellation Rates

Analysis Overview

This visualization examines cancellation rates across different distribution channels used for hotel bookings in Spain. As part of our broader project analyzing Mediterranean hotel dynamics, this analysis highlights which booking methods — such as travel agents, direct websites, or online travel agencies — are most associated with cancellations.

Cancellation Rate by Distribution Channel (Spain)

Key Insight

The bar chart reveals that bookings made through Travel Agents tend to have the highest cancellation rates, while Direct bookings show the lowest. This insight is important for hotel managers looking to reduce booking volatility and improve forecasting — encouraging more direct bookings could lead to fewer cancellations.
